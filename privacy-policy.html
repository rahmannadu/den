1. Introduction

Den Healthcare and Allied Enterprises Private Limited ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal data shared with us through our WhatsApp services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when using our WhatsApp API integration.

2. Information We Collect

When you interact with our WhatsApp services, we may collect the following information:

  • Your phone number
  • Messages you send to us

  • Metadata, such as timestamps and delivery status

We do not store or process sensitive personal data unless explicitly required for the service.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your queries or requests via WhatsApp
  • To provide automated responses and notifications
  • To improve our customer support and services
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data with third parties. However, we may share data with:

  • WhatsApp (Meta) for message delivery and processing
  • Our service providers to facilitate the WhatsApp integration
  • Legal authorities if required by law

We implement security measures to prevent unauthorized access, misuse, or alteration of your data.

5. Data Retention

We retain WhatsApp messages and metadata only for as long as necessary to provide our services. Data may be deleted periodically or upon request.
